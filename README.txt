INSTALLATION: DuplicateDeleter v1.0.0
Launch: Right-click the app and select 'Open' (first time only).
Feature: Safe Delay. Only deletes duplicates older than your chosen number of days.
Safety: Files move to Trash/Recycle Bin. 100% Offline processing.
Security: Automatic log cleanup (30 days) and TLS 1.3 update checks.
